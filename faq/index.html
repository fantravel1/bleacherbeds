Frequently Asked Questions

Your Questions, Answered

Everything you need to know about booking, safety, pricing, facilities, and more.

General Questions

What is BleacherBeds?

BleacherBeds is a platform for ultra-close, ultra-temporary lodging designed for fans traveling to major sporting events, concerts, and festivals. We provide safe, clean, professionally managed overflow sleeping options within walking distance of stadiums and venues when hotels sell out or prices surge.

Where can I sleep near a stadium after a game?

BleacherBeds offers overflow lodging options within walking distance of major stadiums worldwide. Options include converted gyms, university dorms, community centers, pop-up sleep pods, and fan village structures—all professionally staffed with security, clean facilities, and clear check-in procedures.

What events does BleacherBeds cover?

BleacherBeds covers major sporting events (Super Bowl, NFL playoffs, NBA/NHL playoffs, NCAA rivalry weekends, Champions League finals, Premier League derbies), multi-day concerts, stadium festivals, and conventions with stadium-scale crowds. We're expanding to cover more events globally each season.

How do I find a bed near my event?

Go to our Events page and search by venue name, city, or event date. You'll see a map with all nearby BleacherBeds options plus the closest hotel, hostel, and legal rental alternatives. Click on any facility to see full details including price, bed type, and amenities.

Pricing & Booking

How much does BleacherBeds cost?

BleacherBeds is designed to be significantly cheaper than surge-priced hotels during major events. Prices vary by location, facility type, and event demand, but typically range from 50-75% less than hotel prices during peak demand. Every listing shows the price and compares it to the closest hotel.

Can I book online?

Yes. Find your event, select a facility, choose your bed type, and book instantly through our platform. You'll receive a confirmation email with check-in details, facility address, and staff contact information.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express) and digital payment methods including Apple Pay and Google Pay.

What is your cancellation policy?

Cancellations made 7 days before your stay receive a full refund. Cancellations within 7 days are subject to a 25% cancellation fee. Cancellations within 48 hours are non-refundable. Facility-initiated cancellations receive a full refund plus a $25 credit toward your next stay.

Is there a group booking option?

Yes. Groups of 10 or more can contact our group sales team at groups@bleacherbeds.com for custom pricing and dedicated facility arrangements.

Safety & Policies

Is BleacherBeds safe?

Yes. Every BleacherBeds facility requires security presence, check-in identity verification, a clear code of conduct, safe storage for belongings, and emergency procedures. All facilities are professionally managed, inspected, and compliant with local regulations. See our full Safety Standards page for details.

Do I need to show ID to check in?

Yes. Every guest checks in with photo ID. This is a non-negotiable safety requirement. We maintain records of all guests for security and accountability purposes.

What is the code of conduct?

Every facility has house rules covering noise hours, behavior, substances, and harassment prevention. Rules vary by facility but typically include quiet hours (usually 10 PM to 8 AM), no weapons, zero-tolerance harassment policies, and respect for shared spaces. Full rules are provided at check-in.

Is there security staff on site?

Yes. Every BleacherBeds facility has trained security staff available 24/7 during your stay. Staff monitor common areas, respond to incidents, and help guests feel safe.

Can I store my belongings?

Yes. Every facility provides secure storage for bags, electronics, and valuables. Storage options vary (lockers, safes, or secure rooms) but are always available to guests.

What if I have a safety concern?

Report it immediately to on-site staff or security. If you want to report after your stay, email safety@bleacherbeds.com with details. We investigate every report and can immediately suspend facilities with confirmed safety violations.

Bed Types & Facilities

What types of beds does BleacherBeds offer?

BleacherBeds offers several bed types: Dorm Rooms (private or shared university dorms), Sleep Pods (modular pods with privacy screens), Cots & Bunks (in converted gyms and community centers), and Fan Villages (camp-style structures with shared facilities).

What amenities are included?

Amenities vary by facility but typically include clean linens, working bathrooms and showers, secure storage, common areas, and 24-hour security. Specific amenities are listed on each facility's page before you book.

Can I choose my bed type?

Yes. When you search for an event, you'll see all available bed types at nearby facilities. Filter by bed type to see your preferred options, then book the one that works best for you.

Are bathrooms and showers provided?

Yes. Every BleacherBeds facility has working bathrooms and showers available to guests. Shower times and availability vary by facility—check your facility page for details.

Can I bring my own sleeping bag or blanket?

Yes. Clean linens are provided, but you can bring your own blanket or sleeping bag if you prefer. Many guests do.

Do facilities provide food or breakfast?

Some facilities provide complimentary breakfast or snacks, but not all. Check the specific facility page to see what's included. Many facilities are located near restaurants and food options.

Check-In & Check-Out

What time can I check in?

Check-in times vary by facility, but typically begin at 2 PM on your arrival date. Early check-in is sometimes available—ask at the desk when you book.

What time do I need to check out?

Standard check-out is 11 AM on your departure date. Late checkout may be available for an additional fee—contact the facility directly to request.

What do I need to bring to check in?

Bring a valid photo ID, your booking confirmation (digital or printed), and a payment method if you're paying any fees at check-in. Your confirmation email will have the facility's specific requirements.

What if I arrive after dark?

Staff are available 24/7. Contact the facility with your estimated arrival time so they can prepare and ensure safe entry. Call the number provided in your confirmation email.

What if I need to leave early or stay longer?

Contact the facility directly. They may be able to accommodate early checkout or extend your stay based on availability. Early checkout doesn't get a refund, but extensions are available pending availability.

Getting to the Venue

How far is the facility from the stadium?

Every BleacherBeds facility is within walking distance of the venue (typically 5-20 minutes on foot). The exact distance and walk time are shown on the facility page and the map.

What's the walking route like at night?

We verify that walking routes are safe and well-lit. Facility pages include walking safety information and recommended routes. Always travel in groups when possible, especially late at night.

Can I take transportation to/from the venue?

Yes. Most facilities are accessible by rideshare (Uber, Lyft), public transit, or taxi. Some facilities have parking available for an additional fee.

Is parking available?

Parking varies by facility. Check the specific facility page to see if parking is offered and what the fee is.

The Closest Real Bed

What is the "Closest Real Bed"?

Every BleacherBeds event listing includes one "Closest Real Bed" option: the single nearest traditional lodging (hotel, hostel, or legal short-term rental). This gives you the closest option to the venue, whether you prefer traditional or overflow lodging. See our Closest Bed page for more.

Why do you include the closest hotel if you're a competitor?

Because our mission is to give fans the closest possible bed to their event—not just the cheapest overflow bed. Some fans prefer traditional hotels, and we think everyone deserves to know their actual closest option.
