Frequently Asked Questions

What is BleacherBeds?
BleacherBeds is a platform for ultra-close, ultra-temporary lodging designed for fans traveling to major sporting events, concerts, and festivals. We provide safe, clean, professionally managed overflow sleeping options within walking distance of stadiums and venues when hotels sell out or prices surge.
Where can I sleep near a stadium after a game?
BleacherBeds offers overflow lodging options within walking distance of major stadiums worldwide. Options include converted gyms, university dorms, community centers, pop-up sleep pods, and fan village structures — all professionally staffed with security, clean facilities, and clear check-in procedures.
Is BleacherBeds safe?
Yes. Every BleacherBeds facility requires security presence, check-in identity verification, a clear code of conduct, safe storage for belongings, and emergency procedures. All facilities are professionally managed, inspected, and compliant with local regulations.
How much does BleacherBeds cost compared to hotels?
BleacherBeds provides affordable overflow lodging designed to be significantly cheaper than surge-priced hotels during major events. Every event listing includes the closest hotel option alongside more affordable BleacherBeds overflow alternatives within walking distance.
What types of beds does BleacherBeds offer?
BleacherBeds offers several bed types including cots, sleep pods, bunk beds, and dorm rooms. Facilities range from converted gyms and university dorms to modular pop-up sleep pods and fan village camp-style structures — all clean, safe, and professionally managed.
What events does BleacherBeds cover?
BleacherBeds covers major sporting events (Super Bowl, NFL playoffs, NBA/NHL playoffs, NCAA rivalry weekends, Champions League finals, Premier League derbies), multi-day concerts, stadium festivals, and conventions with stadium-scale crowds.
