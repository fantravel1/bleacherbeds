The Problem We Solve

  • Hotels sell out. Major events fill every room within 20 miles. Fans either drive hours or pay 3x normal rates.
  • Price gouging is legal. When demand spikes, hotels raise prices to $500+/night for rooms that normally cost $80.
  • Transportation after events is chaotic. Ride-shares surge, parking gridlock, highways clogged. Fans get exhausted waiting.
  • Safe alternatives don't exist. Couch-surfing is sketchy. Camping isn't an option. Short-term rentals are either full or priced out.
  • Capacity exists but isn't deployed. Universities have empty dorms. Gyms sit idle on weekends. Community centers are underutilized. But there's no platform connecting them to fans.
