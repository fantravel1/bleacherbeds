House Rules

Community Standards & Facility Policies

Every BleacherBeds facility maintains clear rules to ensure a safe, respectful, and comfortable environment for all guests. Review these standards before your stay.

Please Read Before Your Stay

While BleacherBeds has consistent core standards, individual facilities may have additional rules based on their location and setup. You will receive facility-specific rules with your booking confirmation. Review them carefully and comply with all house rules during your stay.

Violations of house rules can result in immediate removal without refund.

Core House Rules (All Facilities)

Respect & Behavior

  • Zero tolerance for harassment. Any form of harassment, abuse, discrimination, or threatening behavior will result in immediate removal and potential law enforcement involvement.
  • Respect shared spaces. Be courteous to other guests. Keep noise levels reasonable and clean up after yourself in common areas.
  • No weapons. Firearms, weapons, or items designed to cause harm are strictly prohibited.
  • No illegal activity. Illegal drugs, stolen property, or any criminal activity will result in immediate removal and law enforcement notification.

Quiet Hours

  • Quiet hours typically run from 10 PM to 8 AM. Keep noise to a minimum during these hours—no loud conversations, loud music, loud videos, or disruptive behavior.
  • Respect sleeping guests. Even outside quiet hours, keep noise at reasonable levels. Remember that guests in adjacent sleeping areas are trying to rest.
  • Facility-specific variations. Some facilities may have different quiet hours posted at check-in. Follow the posted hours for your facility.

Alcohol & Substances

  • Alcohol policies vary by facility. Some facilities permit alcohol in private sleeping areas but not in common areas. Some prohibit alcohol entirely. Check your facility's specific policy at check-in.
  • No illegal drugs. Illegal drugs, controlled substances, and intoxication to the point of disruption are strictly prohibited.
  • No smoking. Smoking, vaping, and e-cigarettes are prohibited inside all BleacherBeds facilities. Designated outdoor smoking areas may be available—ask staff.

Guests & Visitors

  • Only registered guests can stay overnight. Each sleeping area is assigned to registered guests only. Visitors and non-guests cannot sleep in the facility.
  • Visitors welcome in common areas during limited hours. Most facilities allow day visitors in common areas. Ask staff about visitor policies and hours.
  • No unauthorized occupants. Do not allow unauthorized people to sleep in your assigned area. Violators will be removed without refund.

Belongings & Storage

  • Use secure storage. Leave valuables, electronics, and important items in the secure storage provided. Facilities are not responsible for lost, stolen, or damaged personal belongings.
  • Don't leave valuables unattended. Keep jewelry, wallets, phones, and documents with you or in secure storage at all times.
  • Lock your sleeping area. If your sleeping area has a lock, use it. Don't prop doors open.

Facilities & Amenities

  • Respect shared bathrooms. Keep them clean. Take brief showers during designated times if available.
  • No damage to facilities. Don't damage, deface, or vandalize furniture, walls, or equipment. Guests responsible for damage will be charged repair costs.
  • Return common area items. If you borrow anything from the common area, return it promptly and in the same condition.
  • No cooking in sleeping areas. Food preparation (other than pre-packaged snacks) is prohibited in sleeping areas. Use common kitchens if available.

Check-In & Check-Out

  • Check in promptly. Check in by your facility's stated check-in deadline to hold your reservation.
  • Standard check-out is 11 AM. Leave your sleeping area clean and ready for the next guests by the checkout time. Rooms not vacated by 11 AM may be charged an additional night.
  • Return keys and access cards. Return all keys, access cards, or entry devices before checkout.

Safety & Security

  • Comply with security staff. Follow all instructions from security and facility staff. They're there to keep everyone safe.
  • Report safety concerns immediately. If you witness unsafe behavior or security issues, report them to staff right away.
  • No loitering outside facilities. Don't congregate outside the facility or in parking areas. Move through common areas promptly.

Rule Violations & Enforcement

What Happens If I Violate House Rules?

Minor Violations (Warning)

First-time violations like excessive noise during quiet hours typically result in a warning from staff. Comply with corrections immediately.

Serious Violations (Removal)

Serious violations like intoxication, disruptive behavior, harassment, or damage to facilities result in immediate removal without refund.

Criminal Violations (Law Enforcement)

Illegal activity, weapons, assault, theft, or other crimes result in immediate removal and law enforcement notification.

Platform-Wide Bans

Serious or repeated violations may result in bans from all BleacherBeds facilities. We take safety and community standards seriously.

Specific Facility Policies

Your facility may have additional rules for:

  • Pet policies (service animals vs. emotional support animals vs. regular pets)
  • Shower times and availability
  • Specific quiet hour times
  • Visitor policies and hours
  • Parking rules and fees
  • Breakfast availability and times
  • Common area usage hours
  • Laundry facilities and rules
  • WiFi and technology policies
  • Meal options and dietary accommodations

All facility-specific rules will be clearly posted and explained at check-in. Ask staff if you have questions about any policy.

Special Accommodations

Service Animals & Accessibility

Service animals with proper documentation are always welcome. If you require accessibility accommodations, contact your facility before arrival to ensure your needs are met.

Medical Needs

If you have medical conditions or need special accommodations, inform facility staff at check-in. Staff can help connect you with emergency services if needed.

Dietary Requirements

If food is provided or available, inform staff of dietary restrictions or allergies. Some facilities can accommodate special requests if you provide advance notice.

Accessibility Concerns

Tell us if you have mobility or accessibility needs when booking. Not all facilities are fully ADA-compliant—we can help you find one that meets your needs.

Questions About Our Rules?

If you have questions about house rules or facility policies:

  • Before arrival: Email support@bleacherbeds.com
  • During your stay: Ask facility staff. They can explain rules and help with any issues.
  • After your stay: Contact our support team if you have feedback about rules enforcement or facility policies.
