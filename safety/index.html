Safety First

Safe. Clean. Professionally Managed.

Every BleacherBeds facility is held to strict safety and sanitation standards. We don't compromise on security, cleanliness, or the experience of our guests.

Six Non-Negotiable Safety Standards

1. Security Presence

Every BleacherBeds facility has trained security staff on-site from check-in through checkout. Staff monitor common areas, enforce rules, and respond immediately to concerns.

2. ID Verification Check-In

Every guest checks in with photo ID. No anonymous access. We maintain a full record of who is in the facility at all times for safety and accountability.

3. Clear Code of Conduct

Every facility has written rules covering behavior, noise hours, substance policies, and harassment prevention. Rules are enforced consistently and clearly communicated to all guests.

4. Secure Storage

Lockable storage for bags, electronics, and valuables. We never ask guests to leave gear unattended. Every facility provides secure, monitored storage options.

5. Clean & Inspected

Every facility is professionally cleaned and inspected before each event cycle. Clean linens, maintained bathrooms, proper sanitation, and pest control verified.

6. Emergency Procedures

Emergency protocols, first aid availability, and direct communication with venue operators and local authorities. Staff trained in emergency response and guest safety.

Facility Requirements

Before we list a facility, it must meet all of these standards:

Physical Safety

  • Fire safety compliance (exits, extinguishers, alarm systems)
  • Building code compliance and safety inspections
  • Lighting in all common areas and hallways
  • Working bathrooms and showers
  • Climate control (heating/cooling)

Health & Sanitation

  • Professional cleaning before each event
  • Clean linens and mattresses
  • Proper waste management
  • Pest control verified
  • Hand sanitizer and basic hygiene supplies

Security Infrastructure

  • 24/7 staffing during occupancy
  • Security personnel trained in conflict de-escalation
  • Check-in/check-out procedures with ID verification
  • CCTV in common areas (not sleeping areas)
  • Lockable doors on sleeping areas

Guest Experience

  • Clear signage and wayfinding
  • Contact information for on-site staff
  • Written house rules posted visibly
  • Quiet hours enforced
  • Respectful environment for all guests

Our Verification Process

We verify every facility through multiple methods:

1

Partnership Review

Contract review with facility operators, including safety requirements, staff availability, and guest policies.

2

Physical Inspection

In-person site visit to verify cleanliness, safety features, security staffing, and code compliance before first event.

3

Pre-Event Audit

Full facility audit 48 hours before each event to ensure cleanliness, working systems, and staff readiness.

4

Guest Feedback

Post-event surveys from guests. Safety concerns are investigated immediately and can disqualify a facility.

5

Continuous Monitoring

Ongoing communication with facility staff. Any incident, complaint, or code violation is logged and reviewed.

Tips for Staying Safe at BleacherBeds

  • Use the storage. Every facility provides secure storage. Take advantage of it.
  • Know the staff. Get to know the on-site security team and staff. They're there for your safety.
  • Read the house rules. Every facility has different quiet hours and policies. Know them before you arrive.
  • Report anything unusual. See something, say something. Staff want to know immediately about any concern.
  • Stay aware. During late-night hours, stick with others. Don't leave belongings unattended. Travel in groups if possible.
  • Use transportation safely. Plan your travel to/from the venue. Know the safe routes and lit areas.
  • Respect others. Be mindful of noise and shared spaces. Other guests are trying to get rest like you are.

Have a Safety Concern?

If you experience any safety issue at a BleacherBeds facility—during your stay or after—report it immediately.

During your stay: Talk to on-site staff or security. They can address issues in real time.

After checkout: Contact our safety team directly at safety@bleacherbeds.com with details including the date, facility, and incident.

Emergency: Call 911 for immediate safety threats. Always.

We investigate every report. Facilities with confirmed safety violations are immediately suspended and removed from our platform.
