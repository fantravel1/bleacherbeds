Cookie Policy

Last updated: February 2025

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your device (computer, phone, tablet) when you visit a website. They allow websites to recognize your device and store information about your preferences and behavior.

2. How BleacherBeds Uses Cookies

We use cookies and similar technologies to:

  • Remember your login information and user preferences
  • Understand how you use our website
  • Improve our website and services
  • Personalize your experience
  • Track the effectiveness of our marketing campaigns
  • Prevent fraud and enhance security
  • Analyze traffic and user behavior

3. Types of Cookies We Use

Essential Cookies

These cookies are necessary for the website to function properly. They enable core functionality such as security, network management, and accessibility. These cookies do not require consent and cannot be disabled.

  • Session cookies (remember your login during your visit)
  • Security cookies (prevent fraud and unauthorized access)
  • Preference cookies (remember your language and settings)

Functionality Cookies

These cookies enhance your user experience by remembering choices you've made.

  • Remembering your search history
  • Storing your filters and preferences
  • Keeping items in your booking cart

Analytics Cookies

These cookies help us understand how users interact with our website. They collect anonymous information about which pages are visited, how long you stay, and where you came from.

  • Google Analytics - understands user behavior patterns
  • Hotjar - records user interactions and heatmaps
  • Custom analytics - measures booking funnel performance

Marketing & Advertising Cookies

These cookies track your activity to show you targeted advertising on other websites.

  • Facebook Pixel - for Facebook advertising
  • Google Ads - for Google search and display advertising
  • Retargeting cookies - to show ads for events you've viewed

4. Third-Party Cookies

We use third-party services that place cookies on your device:

  • Google Analytics: Analyzes website usage
  • Stripe: Processes payments securely
  • Facebook: Advertising and tracking
  • Hotjar: User experience analytics

These third parties have their own cookie policies. We encourage you to review them.

5. Cookie Duration

  • Session cookies: Deleted when you close your browser
  • Persistent cookies: Remain on your device for a set period (typically 1-2 years)
  • Analytics cookies: Typically expire after 2 years

6. Managing Your Cookie Preferences

You have control over cookies. You can:

  • Accept all cookies: Allow all cookies through our cookie banner
  • Reject non-essential cookies: Decline analytics and marketing cookies
  • Customize preferences: Choose which types of cookies you allow
  • Delete cookies in your browser: Go to your browser settings

How to Delete Cookies in Popular Browsers:

  • Chrome: Settings → Privacy and Security → Delete browsing data
  • Firefox: Settings → Privacy & Security → Cookies and Site Data
  • Safari: Preferences → Privacy → Manage Website Data
  • Edge: Settings → Privacy, search, and services → Clear browsing data

7. Opt-Out Options

You can opt out of specific tracking:

  • Google Analytics: Install the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on
  • Facebook Tracking: Adjust your Facebook ad preferences
  • Industry Opt-out: Visit the Digital Advertising Alliance opt-out portal

8. Do Not Track (DNT) Signals

Some browsers have a "Do Not Track" (DNT) feature. BleacherBeds respects DNT signals where applicable. If your browser sends a DNT signal, we will limit our use of tracking cookies, though we may still use essential cookies for site functionality.

9. EU & GDPR Compliance

If you are in the European Union, we only place non-essential cookies after you've given explicit consent through our cookie banner. You can withdraw consent at any time by adjusting your preferences.

10. California & CCPA Compliance

Under California law, you have the right to opt-out of the "sale" of your personal information. We do not sell your information, but we do share data with third-party analytics and advertising companies. You can opt-out through our cookie preferences.

11. Changes to This Cookie Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date. Your continued use of the site after changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
