About SoFi Stadium

SoFi Stadium opened in September 2020 and is one of the newest, most state-of-the-art sports venues in the United States. Located in Inglewood, Los Angeles, it's home to the LA Rams and LA Chargers of the NFL.

Getting to SoFi

  • By Car: Located just south of LAX Airport (2 miles). Easy freeway access via I-405.
  • By Public Transit: LA Metro connections available. Light rail planned.
  • By Rideshare: Uber and Lyft readily available from nearby hotels and facilities.

Parking

Stadium parking is expensive ($35+). BleacherBeds facilities within walking distance offer better alternatives. Ride-share recommended.

Nearby Activities

  • Hollywood (15 min drive)
  • Santa Monica Beach (20 min drive)
  • Downtown LA (25 min drive)
  • LAX Airport (2 miles)
