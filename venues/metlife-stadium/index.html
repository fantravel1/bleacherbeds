About MetLife Stadium

MetLife Stadium is one of the largest stadiums in the NFL, located in the Meadowlands complex of East Rutherford, New Jersey. It's home to both the New York Giants and New York Jets. The stadium regularly hosts major events, NFL playoffs, and is a Super Bowl candidate venue.

Getting to MetLife

  • By Car: Exit 16W off New Jersey Turnpike. 10 miles from Manhattan.
  • By Public Transit: NJ Transit bus to MetLife Stadium. Parking and ride services available.
  • By Rideshare: Uber/Lyft from NYC or nearby areas (20-40 min from Manhattan).

Parking & Transportation

Stadium parking is $25-40. Many guests use rideshare or NJ Transit for budget-friendly options. BleacherBeds facilities are walkable or accessible by short transit rides.

Nearby Activities

  • New York City (10 miles / 30 min via transit)
  • Meadowlands shopping and entertainment
  • Secaucus retail outlets
  • Hudson River waterfront (Hoboken/Jersey City)
