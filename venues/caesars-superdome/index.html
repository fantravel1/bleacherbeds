About Caesars Superdome & New Orleans

The Caesars Superdome (formerly Mercedes-Benz Superdome, Superdome, and Louisiana Superdome) is an American football stadium located in the Central Business District of New Orleans. It's home to the New Orleans Saints and has hosted countless major events including Super Bowls, Sugar Bowls, and championship tournaments.

Getting to the Superdome

  • By Car: Interstate 10 to downtown New Orleans. Street parking available but pricey ($10-20). Consider rideshare.
  • By Public Transit: Streetcars and buses serve the Superdome area. $2-3 per ride.
  • By Rideshare: Uber/Lyft to/from the Dome. $5-15 depending on time of day.

New Orleans Nearby

  • French Quarter: 0.5 miles - historic, live music, restaurants, nightlife
  • Bourbon Street: 0.6 miles - famous entertainment district
  • Jackson Square: 0.8 miles - historic landmark, street performers
  • River Walk: 1 mile - Mississippi River waterfront dining and shopping

What to Experience in NOLA

  • World-famous cuisine (beignets, gumbo, po'boys, crawfish)
  • Live jazz and blues music 24/7
  • Festivals and parades throughout the year
  • Historic architecture and neighborhoods
