Partnership Requirements

To become a BleacherBeds partner, your facility must meet these standards:

Safety & Compliance

  • Fire safety compliance and working exit routes
  • Building code compliance verified by inspection
  • Working lighting in all areas
  • Accessible bathrooms and showers
  • Security staff available during occupancy

Cleanliness & Maintenance

  • Professional cleaning before each event
  • Clean linens and mattresses provided
  • Working plumbing, heating, and cooling
  • Pest control verified
  • Regular maintenance and repairs

Operations & Staffing

  • 24/7 staff presence during guest occupancy
  • Trained staff in guest check-in and house rule enforcement
  • Secure storage for guest belongings
  • Lockable sleeping areas (doors, lockers, or secure rooms)
  • Check-in/check-out procedures

Guest Experience

  • Clear signage and house rules
  • Respectful, harassment-free environment
  • Enforcement of quiet hours and conduct policies
  • Accessibility accommodations where possible
  • Professional, friendly staff demeanor

Financial & Legal

  • Business license and liability insurance
  • Ability to provide guest documentation and reporting
  • Compliance with local lodging regulations
  • Agreement to BleacherBeds partnership terms

Revenue Model

BleacherBeds works on a revenue-sharing model. You receive a percentage of every booking made through our platform, minus our commission for marketing, booking management, and support.

Typical structure: Partners receive 60-70% of guest fees, with BleacherBeds retaining 30-40% to cover platform costs, marketing, customer support, and payment processing.

Rates vary based on: Location, facility type, capacity, proximity to major venues, and partnership agreement terms.

Payment: You receive payment via direct deposit within 7 days of event completion.

Partnership FAQ

How much capacity do I need?

There's no minimum. We work with facilities of all sizes‚Äîfrom 10 beds to 1,000+. Smaller facilities can still generate meaningful revenue during major events.

What about liability and insurance?

Partners must carry business liability insurance. We provide guidance on coverage needs. BleacherBeds provides additional insurance on top of yours, but you maintain primary responsibility.

Can I operate the facility differently outside of BleacherBeds partnerships?

Yes. Your facility is yours. Partner with us on specific dates while using the space for other purposes the rest of the time.

What if a guest damages my facility?

We screen guests carefully and enforce behavior standards. If damage occurs, you report it and we handle liability determination and claims management.

How often would I be booked?

That depends on your location and facility type. Major event cities see bookings 5-20+ times per year. Smaller markets might see 2-5 bookings annually.

Can I choose which events to participate in?

Yes. You control your calendar. Accept bookings for events that work for you; decline others. You have full flexibility.

How do you handle taxes?

We provide 1099s for all partnership revenue. You're responsible for your own tax reporting and obligations.
